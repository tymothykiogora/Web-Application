BIRTHDAY NAMES

People in African have interesting traditions in all aspects and to date ,some of them continue on practising and observing those traditions.One traditon that has not been taken over by the western culture is the naming process and some communities in Ghana continue to uphold the practice to date. Akan people of Ghana name their children after the day of the week they were born of which these day names have furter meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.
